# Referral

Share Paystream with friends, earn forever. It's that simple.

## How It Works

When someone uses your referral code:
- **You get:** 50% of their trading fees, forever
- **They get:** 15% off on all their fees

It's a win-win. They save money, you make money. And the best part? It never expires.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Code

Head to the referral dashboard in Paystream and generate your unique referral code. Takes about 10 seconds.

### Step 2: Share It

Send your code (or invite link) to friends, post it on social media, or share it however you want. There's no limit to how many people can use it.

### Step 3: Earn

Every time someone you referred trades, you earn 50% of their fees. Forever. No expiration, no limits.

## The Details

* **Minimum to Claim:** You can claim your earnings once they reach 0.1 SOL
* **Lifetime Rewards:** This isn't a one-time thing - you earn as long as they trade
* **Protocol-Level:** The rewards are built into the protocol, so they're guaranteed
* **Easy Tracking:** See exactly how much you've earned in your dashboard

## Why This Is Great

### For You

- Passive income from people you refer
- No work required after sharing your code
- Earn while you sleep
- Build a small income stream over time

### For Your Friends

- They save 15% on fees immediately
- No downside to using a referral code
- Same great platform, just cheaper

## Real Example

Let's say you refer 10 friends, and they each trade $1,000 worth with $10 in fees:

- They each pay: $8.50 (15% off the $10)
- You earn: $5 from each person = $50 total
- And you keep earning 50% of their fees forever

Refer 100 people? The math gets even better. And since it's lifetime, those earnings compound over time. Sharing Paystream with friends is basically free money - they get a discount, you get a cut of their fees forever, and everyone wins. It's one of those rare situations where being helpful also pays you back.
