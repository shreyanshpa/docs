# Referral

Share Paystream with friends, earn forever. It's that simple.

## How It Works

When someone uses your referral code:
- **You get:** 50% of their trading fees, forever
- **They get:** 15% off on all their fees

It's a win-win. They save money, you make money. And the best part? It never expires.

## Getting Started

### Step 1: Create Your Code

Head to the referral dashboard in Paystream and generate your unique referral code. Takes about 10 seconds.

### Step 2: Share It

Send your code (or invite link) to friends, post it on social media, or share it however you want. There's no limit to how many people can use it.

### Step 3: Earn

Every time someone you referred trades, you earn 50% of their fees. Forever. No expiration, no limits.

## The Details

* **Minimum to Claim:** You can claim your earnings once they reach 0.1 SOL
* **Lifetime Rewards:** This isn't a one-time thing - you earn as long as they trade

The referral program benefits everyone. You get passive income from people you refer - no work required after sharing your code, just earn while you sleep and build a small income stream over time. Your friends save 15% on fees immediately with no downside to using a referral code. For example, if you refer 10 friends who each trade $1,000 worth with $10 in fees, they each pay $8.50 (15% off) while you earn $5 from each person. Refer 100 people and the math gets even better - since it's lifetime, those earnings compound over time. Sharing Paystream with friends is basically free money - they get a discount, you get a cut of their fees forever, and everyone wins.
